Digital Playground

In addition to my professional work, I enjoy experimenting with digital art and interactive web experiences in my free time. This section showcases some of my personal projects that blend creativity with technology.

  • Charlor - A zero-player game where collisions between characters or colors result in cell death.
  • Cubed - a three dimensional 9x9 cube with translucent colored walls.
  • Matrix - what it would be like to live in the cube.
  • Seed of Life - a the sacred geometry that is the root of the mandalas.
  • Mandala - a 12-point mandala..
  • Flower of Life - a 24-point mandala.
  • Tesseract - imagining a four-dimensional cube.
  • Zen - an interactive zen playground.
