Digital Craft

A collection of interactive experiments, visual explorations, and playful applications built with code. Each project represents a blend of technical implementation and creative expression.

Games

  • 3D Game of Life - A three-dimensional twist on Conway's classic cellular automaton.
  • 3-State Game of Life - An evolution of the Game of Life with three states instead of two.
  • Catch the Flying Tater Tot - A silly, fast-paced catching game.
  • Charlor - A zero-player game where collisions between characters or colors result in cell death.
  • Cube of Life - Conway's Game of Life rendered on the faces of a rotating cube.
  • Game of Life - The classic cellular automaton created by John Conway.
  • Nirvana - A meditative zero-player game where the seeker collects mana.

Applications

  • Earth Calendar - A novel approach to calendar visualization and time tracking.
  • Hefty - A minimalist weight tracking widget.

Visuals

  • Cubed - A three-dimensional 9x9 cube with translucent colored walls.
  • Flower of Life - Sacred geometry rendered as a 24-point mandala.
  • Interlace - Interwoven patterns and geometric explorations.
  • Mandala - A 12-point mandala generator.
  • Matrix - An immersive view from inside the cube.
  • Seed of Life - The foundational sacred geometry pattern.
  • Spiral - Hypnotic spiral animations and patterns.
  • Tesseract - A four-dimensional cube visualized in 3D space.
