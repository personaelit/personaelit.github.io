Methodology

My process starts with limitation. Find the boundaries of the system, challenge them when possible, work within them when not. Limitations are the seeds of creative problem solving. When you accept constraints, you discover new pathways for design, problem-solving, and collaboration.

Whether I’m building software or working with a team, my goal is to make space for learning through experimentation, failure, and iteration.

My method is iterative: start small, learn fast, refine constantly. Each cycle builds clarity, confidence, and depth. Over time, repitition become rhythm.
