Step 1 / 5

A simple north star

Four responsibilities, in order

Move through the framework one layer at a time. Each step has a small “signal” you can set to mark that you’re moving forward.

Tip: use ← → to navigate.

Signals

On each slide, set an intention and a confidence level. When you hit Next, you’ll see a small nudge that summarizes your signal.

This doesn’t “score” you—just helps you notice momentum.

Step 1

Take care of yourself.

Protect your energy, health, and craft—so you can show up fully.

Pick 1–2 signals you can do in the next 24 hours.

Micro-prompt

What’s one small action that makes tomorrow easier than today?

Step 2

Take care of your loved ones.

Be present. Be reliable. Be kind in the boring moments.

Aim for “small + consistent,” not “perfect.”

Micro-prompt

Who needs you most this week—and what do they actually need?

Step 3

Take care of your community.

Leave things better than you found them—at work and at home.

If it saves time for others, it’s impact.

Micro-prompt

What would make your team’s day 10% smoother?

Step 4

Take care of the world.

Zoom out. Choose actions that scale goodness beyond your circle.

Pick one leverage point you can touch regularly.

Micro-prompt

What’s one choice you repeat that could be a little more intentional?

Wrap

Your quick check

If something pulls you away from all four, it’s probably a distraction. If it strengthens one without harming the others, it’s probably worth doing.
