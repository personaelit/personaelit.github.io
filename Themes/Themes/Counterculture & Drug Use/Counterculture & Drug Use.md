# Counterculture & Drug Use

Psychedelics and uppers are both fuel and filter: they propel the narrative and tint every perception. Thompson chronicles how the counterculture’s spiritual experimentation devolves into chaotic escapism once the movement collides with commercial Las Vegas. The drugs blur ethical lines (see [[Moral Ambiguity & Law]]) and reality itself (see [[Perception vs Reality]]), creating grotesque counter‑advertisements for the straight world.
