# Perception vs Reality

Casino carpets writhe, policemen morph into lizards, and time dilates—a reminder that consciousness is unstable when saturated with mescaline and adrenochrome. Thompson weaponizes unreliable perception to critique media façades and patriotic myths. The tension between what Duke sees and what *is* links back to the illusory [[American Dream]] and exposes paranoia’s slide into [[Violence & Paranoia]].
