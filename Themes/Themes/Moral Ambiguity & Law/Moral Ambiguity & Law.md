# Moral Ambiguity & Law

Duke poses as a sportswriter, a narcotics cop, and a photographer—all lies that skirt legality while mocking authority. *Fear and Loathing* suggests that when institutions chase profit over principle, distinguishing outlaw from officer becomes impossible. This blurring of lines reinforces the terminal state of the [[Death of the American Dream]] and feeds escalating [[Violence & Paranoia]].
